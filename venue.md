- Closing banquet/dinner: Thursday evening.
- Coffee breaks: two per day.

## Visa Invitation Letters

To request a visa invitation letter, provide:

- Full legal name (as on passport)
- Affiliation and position
- Passport number and country of issue
- Planned travel dates
- Registration confirmation number

Send requests to <a href="mailto:info@ispvp.org">info@ispvp.org</a> with subject line: `ISPVP Visa Letter Request`.
