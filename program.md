## Program Format

- Approximately seven two-hour general sessions.
- Two late-afternoon/evening poster sessions (Wednesday and Thursday).
- Per session: 1-2 invited talks and 3-4 selected oral talks.
- Talk lengths: 30 minutes (invited) and 15 minutes (selected).
